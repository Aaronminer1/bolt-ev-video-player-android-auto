# Safety Warning & Legal Disclaimer

## ⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING — READ BEFORE USE

**DO NOT USE THIS APPLICATION WHILE OPERATING A MOTOR VEHICLE.**

Watching video content while driving is **illegal in most jurisdictions** and is
**extremely dangerous**. It diverts your attention from the road, significantly
increases your risk of causing an accident, and can result in injury, death,
property damage, criminal charges, and civil liability.

**This application is intended for use ONLY when:**
- The vehicle is legally and safely parked with the engine off, OR
- The vehicle is stationary and **you are not the driver**, OR
- You are using the Android Auto Desktop Head Unit (DHU) for development and testing purposes on a computer — not in a real vehicle

**If you are behind the wheel of a moving or running vehicle, do not interact with this application.**
